Here is a recipe that you can use to make 'Pastel de Choclo'

Before we get to the physical recipe, here is some history on delicious dish.

Pastel de choclo is a dish that originated in colonial times, likely by Mapuche.

The meat part of the filling is called pino and it is similar to the meat in Chilean empanadas

Pastel de Choclo uses a special native Chilean corn, it is a very sweet version that has a lot of starch

This starcg allows the corn to hold together like dough

Pastel de Choclo

  • 2 lbs ground beef, 85-90% lean
  • 3 onions, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Crust

  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 12 c (roughly 3lbs) sweet corn, thawed if frozen
  • 1 1/4 c milk
  • 1/4 c cornmeal
  • 2 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp salt

Rest of the filling

  • 3/4 c black olives, whole
  • 1/2 c raisins
  • 4 hard boiled eggs, peeled and chopped
  • Granulated sugar (to top)
